I'm an individual guy who works as a freelance game and web developer, and I occasionally take up graphic designing projects.

I take both game development projects involving Unity 3D and web development projects (Html, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, Node js, Mongo Db, React js) and occasional graphic design projects.

It depends upon the complexity of a certain project, depending on brief requirements, detailed list of functionalities and features, your budget and timeframe, I make an informed estimation of a price, whether it may be a fixed price based on a certain feature or if on an hourly basis. I usually charge 40$/hr for gamedev projects and 30$/hr for web dev projects. I'm open to negotiation so just drop me a message and we can come to a mutually beneficial agreement.

No worries, technology has made communication pretty simple, we can communicate via Twitter, Instagram, email, or via video or voice chat whatever is your preferred way. I can fix a time suited as per your availability. I respond to texts asap, so no need to worry! I'll be communicating the progress of your project regularly.

If I'm not sure about something I will not shy away to ask you what to do, all I need from you is trust and enthusiasm for our journey together in the project :)

The payments are based on a milestone basis, Once I'm clear about your requirements, I break down the project in to small chunks of features, and I update you as I complete a section or a feature. Once we are half way in to the project I would require you to make a deposit of a 50% of the project's budget, and you pay the remaining 50% after you have thoroughly verified the completion of the project. You can pay me via Paypal or Transferwise or if you have any other preffered way I'm open to discussion.

I take up all kinds of projects, be they big or small, only two reasons for which I would reject projects are if I already have a lot on my plate in which case I will let you know when I might be available, now for the second reason, at certain few occasions if I feel that the project is out of the scope for a single individual like me to deliver it in the prescribed timeline I will inform you the same. I wouldn't want to waste your time, money or efforts, I will always be honest with you from the begining to avoid any conflicts of interest in future, rest assured!
